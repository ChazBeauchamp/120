About The Electric Fireflies

The Electric Fireflies is an indie rock band known for their eclectic blend of dreamy synth-pop and raw rock energy. Formed in 2020 in a small garage in Bloomington, Indiana, the band quickly gained a local following with their captivating live performances and unique sound. Their music features catchy hooks, ambient synths, and heartfelt lyrics that resonate with a wide audience.
